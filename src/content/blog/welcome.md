Welcome to **SEARO1 Blog**!

This blog is a personal space dedicated to sharing research notes, engineering insights, and technical essays on software development, systems design, and technology.

Here you'll find posts covering:

- **Software Engineering** · Architecture patterns, best practices, and lessons learned
- **Web Development** · Frontend, backend, and DevOps topics
- **Programming** · Deep dives into languages, tools, and techniques
- **Problem Solving** · Walkthroughs of interesting technical challenges

## Tech Stack

This blog is built with:

- [Astro](https://astro.build) · The web framework
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com) · Utility-first styling
- [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org) · Type-safe JavaScript
